Overview

This comprehensive travel itinerary includes a stay in London, exploration of the English countryside by car, a cruise departing from Southampton, and post-cruise activities in London before returning home. The journey begins with a flight from Los Angeles to London and includes all requested destinations with optimized routing to minimize driving distances.

Attractions

London Attractions

  • Tower of London: Historic castle on the north bank of the River Thames
  • London Bridge: Iconic bridge crossing the River Thames
  • Shakespeare's Globe Theatre: Reconstruction of the Globe Theatre, an Elizabethan playhouse
  • London Eye: Giant observation wheel offering panoramic views
  • Big Ben: Famous clock tower of the Palace of Westminster
  • Palace of Westminster: Meeting place of the House of Commons and the House of Lords
  • Westminster Abbey: Historic Gothic abbey church
  • St. Paul's Cathedral: Anglican cathedral with distinctive dome
  • Buckingham Palace: London residence and administrative headquarters of the monarch
  • St. James Park: Royal park in Westminster
  • Borough Market: One of the largest and oldest food markets in London
  • Piccadilly Square: Famous road junction and public space in London's West End
  • National Gallery: Art museum in Trafalgar Square
  • British Museum: Public museum dedicated to human history, art, and culture
  • Harry Potter Experience: Warner Bros. Studio Tour showcasing the making of Harry Potter films
  • Abbey Road Crossing: Famous pedestrian crossing featured on the Beatles' Abbey Road album cover
  • Covent Garden: Shopping and entertainment hub in London's West End

Countryside Attractions

  • Windsor Castle: Royal residence at Windsor
  • Oxford University: Prestigious university city with historic colleges
  • Diddly Squat Farm Shop: Jeremy Clarkson's farm shop featured in his show
  • The Farmers Dog: Local pub near the Cotswolds
  • Castle Combe: Picturesque village in the Cotswolds
  • Bath: Historic city known for its Roman-built baths and Georgian architecture
  • Roman Baths: Well-preserved thermae in the city of Bath
  • The Jane Austen Centre: Museum dedicated to the famous author who lived in Bath
  • Stonehenge: Prehistoric monument consisting of standing stones
  • The Royal Oak: Historic pub near Stonehenge

Cruise Port Attractions

  • Cork, Ireland: Visit Blarney Castle and kiss the famous Blarney Stone
  • Holyhead, Wales: Gateway to Snowdonia National Park and Anglesey Island
  • Belfast, Northern Ireland: Titanic Belfast museum and Giant's Causeway
  • Glasgow, Scotland: Kelvingrove Art Gallery and Museum, Glasgow Cathedral
  • Orkney Islands, Scotland: Neolithic sites including Skara Brae and Ring of Brodgar
  • Edinburgh, Scotland: Edinburgh Castle, Royal Mile, and the historic Old Town

Notes and Recommendations

  • Consider purchasing advance tickets for popular attractions like the Tower of London, London Eye, and Harry Potter Experience
  • The countryside driving route has been optimized to minimize travel time between destinations
  • Pack for variable weather conditions, as British weather can be unpredictable
  • Keep your passport and travel documents easily accessible throughout the journey
  • Consider purchasing a UK SIM card upon arrival for local data and calls
  • For cruise port days, research shore excursions in advance or plan self-guided tours
  • Some cruise ports require water shuttles, so plan extra time for transfers
  • Bring appropriate clothing for formal nights on the cruise
  • Currency needs: British Pounds for the UK, Euros for Ireland
  • The Jane Austen Centre in Bath often has timed entry tickets, consider booking in advance
  • Abbey Road Crossing is a popular photo spot; be patient and respectful of traffic
  • Bath can be crowded; consider arriving early to see the Roman Baths
  • Save all Airbnb check-in instructions and access codes to your phone for offline access
  • Take photos of your car rental before departing to document its condition
  • Confirm your car rental drop-off location and hours before heading to Southampton
  • Allow extra time to find the Wiltshire Airbnb as it's located outside the village
